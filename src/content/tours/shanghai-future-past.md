## China's window to the future

Shanghai is where China prototypes tomorrow. In five days you'll see the colonial architecture of the Bund, the 400-year-old Yu Garden, and the world's most advanced automated port — all within a 30-minute radius.

### The tech you'll actually see

- **Level-4 autonomous vehicles** operating in a live demo zone
- **The world's largest automated container port** — Yangshan, run almost entirely by AI
- **A fintech campus** where China's cashless payment revolution was designed

### Why Shanghai now

For international travelers, Shanghai is the most accessible entry point to "modern China" — English is widely spoken, the infrastructure is world-class, and the contrast between old and new is everywhere.
