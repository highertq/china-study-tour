## The most dynamic corner of China

The **Greater Bay Area** is the most economically dynamic region on earth right now. In a single triangle — Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Hong Kong — you'll find 2,000 years of trading history, the world's most extreme tech miracle, and a global financial capital.

### What you'll witness

- **Guangzhou** — 2,000 years as China's trading window to the world
- **Shenzhen** — 40 years from fishing village to global tech capital
- **Hong Kong** — 180 years as the meeting point of East and West

### Why this tour works

These three cities sit within an hour of each other by high-speed rail, yet each feels like a different country. Eight days is enough to understand why this corner of China became the most economically dynamic region on earth — and what that means for the rest of the world.

### The big picture

By the end of this tour, you'll understand what the "Greater Bay Area" actually means, and why it's being watched closely as a model (or warning) for the future of connected megaregions everywhere.
