## The other China

Forget everything you know about China. **Hainan is different.**

This is tropical China — palm trees, rainforest, volcanic soil, beaches. It sits at the same latitude as Hawaii, and the Chinese call it exactly that. But Hainan is far more than a beach destination.

### Three things that make Hainan unique

**1. The Li people.** Hainan's indigenous inhabitants, with weaving traditions so refined that UNESCO protects them. They've lived here for over 3,000 years, long before "China" reached this far south.

**2. The rainforest.** Yanoda and Jianfengling are real tropical rainforest — canopy walks, waterfalls, plant species found nowhere else. Most foreign tourists have no idea China has jungle.

**3. The Free Trade Port.** Hainan is mid-transformation into the world's largest free trade port — a bold experiment that will define how China trades with the world for the next generation. You'll see it being built.

### Why go now

Hainan in 2026 is a once-in-a-century transition. In ten years, the old fishing villages and rainforest trails will be much harder to find. This is the moment to see both layers at once.
